About

Every dollar should be able to explain itself

Six years ago I got into digital marketing the way most people do: running ads and hoping the numbers worked out. They didn't, not reliably. So I taught myself to build the plumbing underneath the campaigns, the GA4 and GTM pipelines that track a dollar from the ad click all the way to the register. That's the whole job now. I've run marketing in-house for a sushi restaurant, rebuilt a three-location cafe's digital presence from the ground up, restructured a seven-year-old CRM nobody wanted to touch, and bootstrapped my own bakery brand to $3,000 in revenue with nothing but a phone and a Canva account.
