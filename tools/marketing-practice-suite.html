Help — how this suite works

A quick tour of the suite, plus a few things that aren't obvious at a glance.

The Measurement Console

Pick Measurement Console from the grid above to open it below. It has six tabs: Index (KPI reference), Fit (client-fit matcher), Diagnostics (root-cause diagnosis with worked case files), Plan (client measurement plan), Workbench (ad copy + keywords), and Readout (results narrative). Selections carry across every tab.

The other five consoles

Conversion, Findability, Growth, Content, and Strategic Foundation are fully built but not yet open for public interaction — click any of them to join the waitlist and get an email the moment it launches. Their terminology is still searchable in the Glossary below.

Getting back to the hub

Use the ← All practices button in the toolbar above the console to close it and return to the practice grid.

The icon rail

📋 Organizer — every KPI you've added to the Measurement Console's Plan tab, in one list.
🩺 Doctor — set a campaign goal and it flags any KPI in your plan that pulls toward a different goal.
🌙/☀️ — toggle light/dark for the whole page, including the embedded console.
📚 Glossary — every term across all six practices, searchable and filterable.
🗒️ Notes — a scratchpad for this engagement, auto-saved in this browser. Save writes a real .docx (via a native file-save dialog where your browser supports it), Copy copies everything to your clipboard, and Undo/Redo step back and forward through your edits.
