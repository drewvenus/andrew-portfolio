How Ella Cafe Grew Weekly Revenue 25% Across a 3-Location Rollout

Client Ella Cafe (Plantation · Davie · Coral Springs) Role Contract Digital Marketing Specialist Engagement Oct 2025 – Apr 2026
+25%
Weekly Revenue

Location-wide across all three sites, alongside a new B2B catering pipeline that converted into recurring revenue.

Problem

Ella Cafe was losing organic search traffic to unresolved technical issues: broken links, missing internal links, and incorrect redirects were actively suppressing visibility. The site and menu also predated a push to unify branding across the brand's three locations, and the business had no lead generation motion beyond walk-in retail, leaving catering, a higher-margin, recurring-revenue opportunity, untapped.

Solution

Ran a full technical SEO and UX audit in SEMrush, identifying and resolving every broken link, missing internal link, and misconfigured redirect. Redesigned the website landing page and full menu to align with one unified brand identity across all three locations, supporting consolidation and improving digital-to-in-store conversion readiness. In parallel, launched a B2B lead generation campaign for catering across Meta Ads, LinkedIn Ads, Google Ads, and direct outreach, appeared on-camera to direct and produce branded Instagram Reels, and built a 90-day multi-channel roadmap, content calendar, community management SOP, and a Google Business Profile posting cadence of 2x/week per location, coordinated directly with franchise ownership and on-site staff.
