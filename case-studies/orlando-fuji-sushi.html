How a Single $70 Influencer Reel Returned 4.73× for Orlando Fuji Sushi

Client Orlando Fuji Sushi Role In-House Digital Marketing Specialist Engagement Aug 2022 – Jun 2026
4.73×
ROAS: Influencer Partnership

$4.67 cost-per-new-customer on a single $70 gifted-meal collaboration, one of three paid channels run in parallel. Full breakdown below.

Problem

Fuji Sushi had no attribution system in place: ad spend wasn't tied to actual conversions. A high-ticket menu item, the Sushi Boat, was underperforming at just $480/month, and customer acquisition relied heavily on foot traffic from its location rather than a measurable, repeatable channel. The restaurant needed to prove what was working across every channel it tried, not just run more ads.

Solution

Built a custom GA4 and Google Tag Manager attribution pipeline from scratch to track every dollar of spend down to the conversion. Ran three parallel acquisition channels, Google Ads, a paid Instagram follower campaign, and a gifted-meal influencer partnership, each measured independently rather than blended into one vague number. In parallel, repositioned the underperforming Sushi Boat through new copy, signage redesign, and short-form video, and layered in SMS and email retention systems so new customers had a path back to becoming repeat customers.
